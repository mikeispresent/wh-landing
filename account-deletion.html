Delete Your Account.

How to delete your WildHeavy account and what happens to your data

01 · How to Delete Your Account.

You can delete your WildHeavy account at any time from within the app. The process is immediate and irreversible.

Steps to delete from the app

  1. Open WildHeavy and go to your Profile tab
  2. Tap the gear icon to open Account Settings
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap "Delete My Account"
  4. Confirm by typing "DELETE" when prompted
  5. Your account will be permanently deleted
Note: If you cannot access the app, you can request account deletion by emailing support@wildheavy.com from the email address associated with your account. We will process your request within 48 hours.

02 · What Happens When You Delete.

Account deletion is permanent. Once confirmed, the following actions are taken immediately:

Permanently removed

  • Your profile information (bio, avatar, display name, location)
  • Your email address and authentication credentials
  • Your social connections (follows, followers)
  • Your saves, likes, and notification history
  • Your private location data and preferences
  • Your dietary restrictions and taste profile

Anonymized (no longer linked to you)

  • Rankings you published are attributed to "Deleted User"
  • Menu cards you created are attributed to "Deleted User"
  • Your contributions continue to count toward aggregate dish scores, but cannot be traced back to your identity
Why anonymization instead of full removal? WildHeavy's community rankings rely on contributions from many users. Removing individual data points from aggregate scores would compromise the accuracy of recommendations for everyone. By anonymizing rather than deleting this content, we preserve community value while ensuring your personal identity is completely and permanently severed from the data. Your email is replaced with a non-recoverable placeholder. There is no way to reconnect the anonymized content to you.

03 · After Deletion.

Once your account is deleted:

  • You will be immediately signed out of the app
  • Your username will become available for others to claim
  • You cannot recover your account or any of its data
  • If you'd like to use WildHeavy again, you'll need to create a new account

04 · Questions?

If you have questions about account deletion or your data, contact us at support@wildheavy.com.

For full details on how we handle your data, see our Privacy Policy.
