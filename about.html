The Why

Why we built this.

You know the friend who always knows what to order. The one you text before every reservation. WildHeavy is built for them. For the sommelier with a list of wines nobody's finding on their own, and the bartender who can tell you which dish is extraordinarily perfected that night.

It's a place where all that knowledge finally lives together. Industry pros, food obsessives, and curious regulars sharing the same table. Hospitality voices carry more weight in our rankings because expertise matters. Great taste lives everywhere, and the platform is built to surface it wherever it shows up. Welcome to the Regular's Advantage.

The System

How it works.

  1. i.
    Find the dish.
    Best burger in the city? Top Rank has it. Community rankings weighted by expertise, so the answer comes from pros and regulars who've actually done the research. Find the move, then go.
  2. ii.
    Find the visit.
    Your friend had dinner at Bavette's last Tuesday. Instead of decoding an Instagram story or texting "wait, what did you order," pull up their Menu Card. Every dish, every rating, the full play-by-play. No lost saves, no forgotten recs.
  3. iii.
    Build the night.
    Not just restaurants. Dinner before the show, cocktails after, the late-night spot to close it out. Pull in Google Places or WildHeavy-verified restaurants, see the travel between each stop, and share the whole night in one link. That's a Hot Route.
  4. iv.
    Call your shot.
    Post a Signal when you're looking for company at a rez, or let your regulars know you're on shift tonight.
