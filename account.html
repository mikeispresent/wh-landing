Account

Your Account.

Manage your profile, preferences, and settings

Your account is where you manage everything in WildHeavy. Access the full suite of features from the mobile app.

01 · What You Can Do Here.

Open the WildHeavy app to access your account dashboard and manage:

  • Your profile bio, avatar, and public location
  • Your dining preferences and dietary restrictions
  • Your ranking history and saved cards
  • Your followers and following list
  • Notification preferences
  • Privacy and account security settings
  • Verification status (if applicable)

02 · Accessing Your Account.

To manage your account:

  1. Open the WildHeavy app on your device
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom
  3. Tap the gear icon to open Account Settings
  4. Browse and update your preferences
Note: Most account management features are available exclusively in the mobile app for the best experience. Visit TestFlight to download WildHeavy.

03 · Account Security.

Keep your account safe by:

  • Using a strong password (at least 10 characters)
  • Not sharing your credentials with others
  • Enabling two-factor authentication if available
  • Logging out of sessions you no longer use
Suspicious activity? If you notice unauthorized access or suspicious activity on your account, change your password immediately and contact security@wildheavy.com.

04 · Your Privacy.

Control what's visible to others by managing:

  • Your public profile visibility
  • Who can follow you and see your saves
  • Your ranking attribution and anonymity preferences
  • Whether your location is publicly displayed

For a complete overview of how we handle your data, see our Privacy Policy.

05 · Need to Delete Your Account?

You can permanently delete your WildHeavy account at any time. The process is immediate and irreversible. For step-by-step instructions, visit our Account Deletion page.

06 · Help & Support.

Have questions about your account or need assistance?

Contact us at support@wildheavy.com.
